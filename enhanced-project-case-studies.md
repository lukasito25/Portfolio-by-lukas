**Market Positioning & Growth:**

- Launched a professionally designed brand website with a comprehensive SEO optimization strategy

- Developed go-to-market strategies that positioned StagStrat as a credible player in institutional trading

**Operational Excellence:**

- Streamlined development processes to dramatically reduce feature lead times
- Established agile methodologies and cross-functional collaboration frameworks
- Implemented data-driven decision-making processes for feature prioritization

### Results

- **80% reduction in feature lead time** - Transformed development velocity through optimized processes and clear prioritization
- **40% increase in organic traffic** - SEO strategy successfully established strong digital presence for brand awareness
- **Complete product roadmap delivery** - Successfully aligned all product initiatives with strategic business objectives
- **API integration prioritization** - Delivered market-relevant features based on comprehensive user research and competitive analysis

### Skills Demonstrated

- **Strategic Product Leadership:** End-to-end product lifecycle ownership for complex fintech platform

The challenge included modernizing the entire tech stack, improving performance dramatically, and coordinating complex international team dynamics.

### Approach

**Cross-Functional Leadership:**

- Led development teams of 10+ members distributed across Colombia, Netherlands, and Germany
- Established unified communication protocols and project management frameworks for seamless international collaboration
- Implemented agile sprint planning and product backlog management to ensure consistent delivery across time zones

**Technical Transformation:**

- Orchestrated complete migration from legacy technology to modern Next.js and React architecture

**Product Management Excellence:**

- Utilized advanced Agile methodology for sprint planning and feature delivery
- Coordinated user research and stakeholder feedback integration throughout the redesign process

### Results

- **55% improvement in page load speed** - Technical optimization delivered measurable performance gains for millions of users
- **Successful international team leadership** - Effectively managed 10+ team members across 3 countries and multiple time zones
- **Zero-downtime migration** - Complex technical transformation completed without service interruption
- **Modern tech stack implementation** - Successfully migrated to Next.js and React for improved scalability and maintainability
- **Enhanced user experience** - Complete UI/UX overhaul improved engagement and user satisfaction

### Skills Demonstrated

- **International Team Leadership:** Cross-cultural team management across Colombia, Netherlands, and Germany
- **Technical Product Management:** Complex tech stack migration and performance optimization
- **Agile Methodology:** Advanced sprint planning, backlog management, and cross-functional coordination
